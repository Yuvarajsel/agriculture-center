BENEFITS OF USING IOT IN SMART IRRIGATION

    Water Conservation

  • IoT-based sensors monitor soil moisture, weather conditions, and humidity in real time.
  • Automated irrigation systems ensure that crops receive the right amount of water, reducing water wastage.
  • Helps in areas facing water scarcity by using water efficiently.

    • Cost Savings

  • Reduces electricity and labor costs by automating irrigation schedules.
  • Farmers spend less on water and fertilizers due to precise irrigation control.
  • Early detection of leaks prevents unnecessary water bills.

    • Increased Crop Yield

  • Ensures optimal water supply to crops, improving growth and productivity.
  • Reduces overwatering and underwatering, preventing crop damage.
  • Monitors soil nutrients, ensuring better-quality crops.
